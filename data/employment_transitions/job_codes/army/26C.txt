Job Code: 26C

Description:
Performs or supervises intermediate and depot level support maintenance on target acquisition and ground surveillance radars and associated equipment.

Supervises the performance of intermediate and depot level maintenance, calibration, and installation of communications-electronic equipment; provides advice and makes, recommendations to the commander aid staff on all matters pertain to enlisted personnel and their families; accompanies the commander on inspections and visits and at ceremonies; assists in inspection of command activities facilities aid personnel as prescribed by the commander; holds First Sergeants' or Sergeants' Major call to announce information and instructions; insures that newly assigned enlisted personnel are instructed in military courtesy, customs, of the service, and command regulations or policies; provides counsel and guidance to NCOs and other enlisted personnel of the command; inspects duties performed by subordinate NCOs; notes discrepancies and initiates appropriate corrective action; assists in reception of visitors to the command; sits as president or, member of command promotion board for NCOs, as authorized by regulations; performs other duties prescribed by, the commander.

Performs intermediate and depot level maintenance on target acquisition/surveillance radars and associated equipment; provides technical guidance and limited supervision for lower grade personnel involved in maintaining target acquisition/surveillance radar and associated equipment; supervises the performance of intermediate and depot level maintenance on target acquisition/surveillance radars and associated equipment.

Supervises the performance of intermediate and depot level maintenance, calibration, and installation of communications-electronic equipment.
