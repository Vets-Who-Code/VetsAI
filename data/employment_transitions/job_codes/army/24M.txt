Job Code: 24M

Description:
Supervises and performs unit level maintenance on the VULCAN weapon system and associated equipment.

Performs unit level maintenance on the VULCAN weapon system, range-only radar, and associated equipment; troubleshoots, performs scheduled maintenance, checks, tests, adjusts, and isolates malfunctions on components of the VULCAN weapon system and associated subsystems; performs maintenance on unit test, measurement, and diagnostics equipment; initiates requests for repair parts; provides technical assistance and guidance in the conduct of operational and maintenance inspections; supervises the training of maintenance personnel; instructs maintenance personnel in maintenance procedures and troubleshooting techniques; maintains forms for scheduled maintenance of VULCAN system and test equipment.

Establishes maintenance procedures; conducts technical inspections on VULCAN weapon system and associated equipment; coordinates maintenance activities with supporting units; supervises the requisitioning and stockage of supplies and repair parts.

The senior electronic maintenance chief plans and directs electronic maintenance operations at all levels of command and echelons of the Army; performs electronic maintenance staff functions; provides technical advice to commanders and staff concerning Army electronic maintenance and electronic logistic support matters; writes directives, policies and procedures which establishes Army electronic maintenance requirements; serves as senior staff noncommissioned officer in electronic maintenance school staff.
