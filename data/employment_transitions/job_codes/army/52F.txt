Job Code: 52F

Description:
Performs and supervises unit, direct support and general support maintenance functions.

Supervises the performance of unit, direct support and general support (DS/GS) special purpose equipment maintenance activities; performs maintenance management activities, including production and quality control; provides technical guidance and training; the mechanical maintenance supervisor supervises, plans, coordinates, and directs the unit, direct support and general support (DS/GS) maintenance of all mechanical equipment; (1) Serving as principal maintenance or operations NCO in maintenance battalion or higher level organization; (2) Supervising personnel performing the duties of (a) Machinist (44E4O); (b) Armament/fire control maintenance supervisor (45Z4O); (c) Construction equipment repairer (62B4O); (d) Track vehicle repairer (63H4O).

Performs maintenance of turbine driven generators and associated items such as the Aviation Ground Power Unit (AGPU), Medical Unit Self-Contained Transportable (MUST), Electric Power Plant II (EPP II) and generator set Aviation 10KW 28V (10/28); repairs or overhauls components and assemblies, such as starters, turbine driven generators, fuel pumps, exciters, voltage regulators, governors, switches, and control circuits, etc.; supervises activities of a section performing unit or DS/GS maintenance on turbine engine driven generator sets, AGPU, MUST, EPP II and generator set Aviation 10KW 28V (10/28).

The mechanical maintenance supervisor supervises, plans, coordinates, and directs the unit, direct support and general support (DS/GS) maintenance of all mechanical equipment; (1) Serving as principal maintenance or operations NCO in maintenance battalion or higher level organization; (2) Supervising personnel performing the duties of (a) Machinist (44E4O); (b) Armament/fire control maintenance supervisor (45Z4O); (c) Construction equipment repairer (62B4O); (d) Track vehicle repairer (63H4O).
