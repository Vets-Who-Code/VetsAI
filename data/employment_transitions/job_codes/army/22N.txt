Job Code: 22N

Description:
Supervises or performs direct and general support maintenance on the NIKE-HERCULES System Missile and launcher and their associated equipment.

Supervises and coordinates support level maintenance of the Improved Nike Hercules Guided Missile System to include the Missile, Launcher Control, Launcher and associated equipment, radars (LOPAR, HIPAR, TTR, TRR, AND MTR), Director-Station Group, Tracking Station Group; radar Target Simulator (Shops 1, 2, and 3), and other systems associated items and test equipment; serves as section chief or principal noncommissioned officer of a platoon performing missile system support maintenance; assists in development and supervises quality assurance/quality control program; performs duties shown in preceding level skill; serves as principal NCO in unit at battalion level or higher performing DS/GS maintenance on the NIKE missile system; serves as First Sergeant of a company; performs duties associated with SQI M; serves as chief instructor in a training facility, chief adviser to a Reserve Component unit, or chief adviser to a foreign military unit.

Performs duties shown in preceding level skill; serves as principal NCO in unit at battalion level or higher performing DS/GS maintenance on the NIKE missile system; serves as First Sergeant of a company; performs duties associated with SQI M; serves as chief instructor in a training facility, chief adviser to a Reserve Component unit, or chief adviser to a foreign military unit; provides advice and makes, recommendations to the commander aid staff on all matters pertain to enlisted personnel and their families; accompanies the commander on inspections and visits and at ceremonies; assists in inspection of command activities facilities aid personnel as prescribed by the commander; holds First Sergeants' or Sergeants' Major call to announce information and instructions; insures that newly assigned enlisted personnel are instructed in military courtesy, customs, of the service, and command regulations or policies; provides counsel and guidance to NCOs and other enlisted personnel of the command; inspects duties performed by subordinate NCOs; notes discrepancies and initiates appropriate corrective action; assists in reception of visitors to the command; sits as president or, member of command promotion board for NCOs, as authorized by regulations; performs other duties prescribed by, the commander.

Performs support level maintenance on the NIKE-HERCULES system missile and associated handling equipment and electronic, mechanical, and hydraulic test equipment; provides technical guidance to lower grade personnel; supervised duties shown in preceding levels of skills.
