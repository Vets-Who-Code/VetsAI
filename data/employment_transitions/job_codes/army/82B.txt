Job Code: 82B

Description:
Performs construction surveys to determine relative positions of points on the ground to provide data for construction.

Participates in performance of construction surveys; directs survey party and works independently while conducting construction surveys; supervises technical engineering relating to construction surveying, drafting, and testing of construction materials.

Plans, directs, and performs technical site development activities; provide staff supervision and principal noncommissioned officer direction-to units engaged in structural building and repair, or provision of water and utilities services; provide general supervision to personnel performing duties of Technical engineering supervisor (51T40); Firefighter (51M40); Construction' engineering supervisor (51H40); Diver (00B40); Construction equipment supervisor (62N40); Transmission and distribution specialist (52G40); Prime power production specialist. (52E40); Industrial gas production specialist (53B40) Reserve forces only; perform principal noncommissioned officer duties associated with SQI M.
