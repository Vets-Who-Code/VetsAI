Job Code: 55R

Description:
Supervises, performs or assists in ammunition stock control and accounting duties.

Performs ammunition supply stock control and accounting duties using both automated and manual procedures; performs manual and automated stock management related duties and supervises performance of ammunition stock control and accounting duties.

Supervises ammunition storage platoon receipt, stockage and issue operations; apply doctrine, regulations, and directives to ammunition operations; supervise or advise on ammunition packaging and rigging loads for movement by all types of transportation; inspect operations and area to evaluate safety and efficiency of work; institute corrective action; supervise ammunition related ADP operations; perform principal noncommissioned officer duties associated with SQI M; perform the duties of a 4 skill level MOSC in CMF 55; assist in surveying allotted areas for establishment of ammunition installations, including administrative areas; lay out and evaluate facilities for ammunition storage operations; recommend employment of technical assistance teams; plan requirements for labor, equipment, and supplies; instruct in application of work policies and procedures; assist in preparation of plans, policies, and procedures; prepare or review evaluations and reports on operational training matters; analyze ammunition stock with automatic data processing assistance.
