Job Code: 352K

Description:
Manages personnel and equipment assets in establishment and employment of non-Morse intercept activities.

Performs duties in preceding skill levels; provides technical guidance and direction to subordinate intelligence elements; utilizes of non-Morse personnel and equipment by the allocations of all non-Morse resources; manages non-Morse related SIGINT systems to ensure cost and technically effective life-cycle training support is planned and provided for these systems.

Performs duties in preceding skill levels; provides guidance and technical input to subordinate units and other intelligence activities and staff elements; manages non-Morse resources to the best possible utilization and provides guidance to DA on its use.

Manages personnel and equipment assets in establishment and employment of non-Morse intercept activities; plans, coordinates, and supervises activities of personnel engaged in these operations, establishes work schedules and priorities, and evaluates performance of subordinates; coordinates collection activities with applicable traffic analysis and cryptanalysis personnel; plans and coordinates procedures for the performance of maintenance calibration, adjustment, and test of non-morse intercept personnel and equipment; establishes, directs, and evaluates qualification training programs for non-Morse operations.
