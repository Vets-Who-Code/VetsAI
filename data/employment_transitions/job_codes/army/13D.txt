Job Code: 13D

Description:
Directs and supervises deployment of artillery controlled sensing devices.

Commands or assists commander of field artillery target acquisition units; directs and supervises deployment of artillery controlled sensing devices, ranging systems, and meteorological elements; develops and identifies targets for employment of fire support systems; serves as a staff officer at higher headquarters or as a service school instructor.
