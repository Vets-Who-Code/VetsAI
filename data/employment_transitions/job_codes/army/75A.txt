Job Code: 75A

Description:
Examination and testing, of subsistence for wholesomeness, nutritional quality, and conformance with other Federal and military standards.

Performs, supervises, directs or coordinates inspection, to include examination and testing, of subsistence for wholesomeness, nutritional quality, and conformance with other Federal and military standards; provides veterinary medical diagnostic and treatment services and surgery for Government-owned animals and other animals as authorized; participates as research investigator in biomedical research and research support; instructs or trains officers and enlisted specialists in any or all of these aspects of military veterinary services; this is an initial/entry level skill for the AOC 64A.
