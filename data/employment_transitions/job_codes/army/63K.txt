Job Code: 63K

Description:
Treat abnormalities and defects of teeth for children from birth through adolescence.

Examines, diagnoses and treats or prescribes treatment for diseases, abnormalities, injuries and defects of teeth and supporting structures for children from birth through adolescence and special patients of any age who demonstrate mental, physical and/or emotional handicaps.
