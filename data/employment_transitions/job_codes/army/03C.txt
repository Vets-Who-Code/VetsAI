Job Code: 03C

Description:
Supervises or conducts sports and physical activities programs.

Conducts sports and physical activities programs; supervises operation of sports and physical activity programs.

Supervises performance of administrative functions; provides advice and makes, recommendations to the commander aid staff on all matters pertain to enlisted personnel and their families; accompanies the commander on inspections and visits and at ceremonies; assists in inspection of command activities facilities aid personnel as prescribed by the commander; holds first sergeants' or sergeants' major call to announce information and instructions; insures that newly assigned enlisted personnel are instructed in military courtesy, customs, of the service, and command regulations or policies; provides counsel and guidance to NCOs and other enlisted personnel of the command; inspects duties performed by subordinate NCOs; notes discrepancies and initiates appropriate corrective action; assists in reception of visitors to the command; sits as president or, member of command promotion board for NCOs, as authorized by regulations; performs other duties prescribed by, the commander.

Supervises operation of physical activities and sports programs and serves as principal NCO in recreational services staff element or activity; supervises performance of administrative functions.
