Job Code: 24V

Description:
Supervises intermediate maintenance of HAWK missile system.

Serves as maintenance chief in unit at battalion or higher level; serves as first sergeant of a company; performs duties associated with SQI M; serves as chief instructor in a training facility, chief advisor to a Reserve Component unit, or chief advisor to a foreign military unit.

Serves as a section chief or principal noncommissioned officer of a platoon performing missile system support maintenance; supervises and inspects activities of subordinate noncommissioned officers engaged in missile system maintenance and associated supply activities; assists in development and supervises quality assurance and quality, control program; serves as maintenance chief in unit at battalion or higher level; serves as First Sergeant of a company; performs duties associated with SQI M; serves as chief instructor in a training facility, chief advisor to a Reserve Component unit, or chief advisor to a foreign military unit.
