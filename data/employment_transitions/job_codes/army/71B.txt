Job Code: 71B

Description:
Conducts and directs the performance of biochemical analysis.

Conducts and directs the performance of biochemical analysis and investigates metabolic processes occurring in organisms; performs experiments and conducts research to determine how a body reacts/adapts to a range of military environments; duties are performed in MTOE/TDA organizations, both CONUS and OCONUS.
