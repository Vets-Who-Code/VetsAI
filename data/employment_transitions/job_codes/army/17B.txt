Job Code: 17B

Description:
Supervises or participates in operation of counterfire and moving target radars and associated equipment.

Leads in combat operations of the radar platoon; the FA senior sergeant leads and supervises the operation of the unit command post in accordance with command directives; leads and supervises the preparation and distribution of maps, operational information, operation reports, and training material; leads, supervises, and participates in coordination and implementation of cannon, missile, rocket or target acquisition operations, training programs, administrative matters, and communications activities, providing tactical and technical guidance to subordinates, and professional support to lower and higher grade soldiers in the accomplishment of their duties; supervises the maintenance of staff journals, files, records, and reports; performs principal NCO duties for SQI M; plans, coordinates, supervises, and participates in the collection, interpretation, analyses, and evaluation of intelligence and target acquisition activities and data; supervises and maintains classified files and records; supervises processing of individual security clearances; supervises and participates in developing targets from target acquisition information; plots, records, and reports targets; serves as principal NCO of FA battalion, brigade, division artillery, or corps artillery operations activity and supervises the processing of operations and intelligence information; prepares operational SOP; monitors, inspects, and evaluates FA training programs; leads, supervises, and participates in identifying and indicating location, strength, tactical deployment, and emplacement of enemy units; reads and interprets maps and aerial photographs; keeps situation maps, charts, and overlays.
