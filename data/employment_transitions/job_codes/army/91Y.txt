Job Code: 91Y

Description:
Supervises or conducts routine diagnostic tests and assists in the care and treatment of ophthalmology or optometry patients.

Assists in the management of large ophthalmology or optometry clinic; must be qualified in an MOS in CMF 91 (Medical) at the 4 skill level; serves as principal NCO of staff section, hospital, other medical facilities or service team.

Must be qualified in an MOS in CMF 91 (Medical) at the 4 skill level; serves as principal NCO of staff section, hospital, other medical facilities or service team; provides advice and makes, recommendations to the commander aid staff on all matters pertain to enlisted personnel and their families; accompanies the commander on inspections and visits and at ceremonies; assists in inspection of command activities facilities aid personnel as prescribed by the commander; holds First Sergeants' or Sergeants' Major call to announce information and instructions; insures that newly assigned enlisted personnel are instructed in military courtesy, customs, of the service, and command regulations or policies; provides counsel and guidance to NCOs and other enlisted personnel of the command; inspects duties performed by subordinate NCOs; notes discrepancies and initiates appropriate corrective action; assists in reception of visitors to the command; sits as president or, member of command promotion board for NCOs, as authorized by regulations; performs other duties prescribed by, the commander.

Conducts routine diagnostic tests and assists in the care and treatment of ophthalmology aid optometry patients; conducts routine diagnostic tests and assists in the care of eye patients; assists in the management of small ophthalmology or optometry clinic.
