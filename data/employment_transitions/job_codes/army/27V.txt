Job Code: 27V

Description:
Performs intermediate maintenance on the HAWK missile systems.

Supervises and coordinates intermediate maintenance on the HAWK launcher loader, transporter, continuous wave radar, high powered illuminator radar, pulse acquisition radar, platoon command post, identification of friend or foe equipment, launching section control box, field maintenance equipment, automatic test equipment, and system associated test equipment.; supervises and coordinates initial maintenance on complex HAWK system interface circuits; supervises lower grade maintenance on complex HAWK missile system and support maintenance equipment.
