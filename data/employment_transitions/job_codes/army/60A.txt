Job Code: 60A

Description:
Plans, establishes, directs and supervises health delivery activities.

Plans, establishes, directs and supervises health delivery activities within military units authorized a surgeon or within research and development, test and evaluation activities concerned with medical aspects of military operations; commands medical unit or installation engaged in furnishing medical care to sick, injured or wounded personnel.
