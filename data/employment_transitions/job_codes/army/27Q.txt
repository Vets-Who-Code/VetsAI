Job Code: 27Q

Description:
Operates, performs, or supervises organizational maintenance on SGT YORKS gun system special design field maintenance test equipment (FMTE).

Duties involve intermediate maintenance on the following weapons systems; PERSHING; Tube-launched Optically-tracked Wire-guided missile(TOW); DRAGON; SHILLELAGH; LANCE; Multiple Launch Rocket System (MLRS); CHAPARRAL; REDEYE; VULCAN; HAWK; ROLAND; duties also involve intermediate maintenance on the Land Combat Support System (LCSS) and Forward Area Alerting Radar (FAAR) system; and associate test, trainer and maintenance checkout equipment.

Duties involve intermediate maintenance on the following weapons systems; PERSHING; Tube-launched Optically-tracked Wire-guided missile(TOW); DRAGON; SHILLELAGH; LANCE; Multiple Launch Rocket System (MLRS); CHAPARRAL; REDEYE; VULCAN; HAWK; ROLAND; duties also involve intermediate maintenance on the Land Combat Support System (LCSS) and Forward Area Alerting Radar (FAAR) system; and associate test, trainer and maintenance checkout equipment; provides advice and makes, recommendations to the commander aid staff on all matters pertain to enlisted personnel and their families; accompanies the commander on inspections and visits and at ceremonies; assists in inspection of command activities facilities aid personnel as prescribed by the commander; holds First Sergeants' or Sergeants' Major call to announce information and instructions; insures that newly assigned enlisted personnel are instructed in military courtesy, customs, of the service, and command regulations or policies; provides counsel and guidance to NCOs and other enlisted personnel of the command; inspects duties performed by subordinate NCOs; notes discrepancies and initiates appropriate corrective action; assists in reception of visitors to the command; sits as president or, member of command promotion board for NCOs, as authorized by regulations; performs other duties prescribed by, the commander.

Operates and performs organizational and support level maintenance on SGT YORK sun system special design FMTE; performs unit-under-test (UUT) checkout and repair of SGT YORK gun system radar and fire control (FC) associated components; performs and supervises UUT checkout and repair of SGT YORK gun system radar and FC associated equipment.
