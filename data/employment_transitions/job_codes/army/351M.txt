Job Code: 351M

Description:
Conducts and supervises both tactical and strategic interrogation.

Performs duties in preceding skill levels; provides technical guidance and direction to subordinate elements conducting full spectrum or specific MSO; supervises all interrogation, debriefing, and Human Source Contact Operations (HSCO); manages interrogation, debriefing and HSCO elements throughout all levels of command within the US Army and other agencies.

Performs duties in preceding skill levels; provides guidance and technical input to subordinate interrogator, debriefing and HSCO elements or other staff elements; manages interrogational/debriefing and HSCO elements providing those skills supported from the level of combat commander to DOD level.

Interrogates as defined by regulation, the UCMJ and other applicable regulations and agreements; conducts and supervises both tactical and strategic interrogation related duties to include MSO; supervises the technical performance of subordinate military and civilian personnel in related job skills; develops and approves interrogation, documents, plans, and missions; advises the support element on the best employment of HUMINT Collector assets; coordinates with other intelligence and non-intelligence agencies in the performance of interrogation, debriefing and MSO duties; writes, reviews, and approves interrogation and limited technical intelligence reports.
