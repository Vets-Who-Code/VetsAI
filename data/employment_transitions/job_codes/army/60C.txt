Job Code: 60C

Description:
Plan, coordinate and direct a program designed to maintain health.

Determines status and conditions influencing health of military and appropriate civilian personnel; formulates and recommends measures for health improvements; plans, coordinates and directs a program designed to maintain health, improve physical fitness and prevent disease and injury.
