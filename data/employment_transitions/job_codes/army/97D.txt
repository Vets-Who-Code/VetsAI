Job Code: 97D

Description:
Supervises and performs analysis of intelligence data in conjunctions with counterintelligence and field operations intelligence activities.

Analyzes intelligence data; establishes and keeps counterintelligence and field operations intelligence files; receives, analyzes, and processes correspondence, reports, summaries, and other materials; observe security regulations in processing of intelligence data; assists in development of intelligence estimates, worksheets and plans; reviews requirements instructions, reports, personality files, and general reference works to secure back ground information; compiles summaries by review of intelligence and investigative reports and memorandums; types at a minimum of 25 words per minute.

Must be able to perform the duties of 97D20; supervises analysis of intelligence data; details analysis to specific tasks; instructs analysts in extracting, assembling, filing, collecting, and disseminating intelligence information; establishes and keeps counterintelligence and field operations intelligence files, including case control files, suspense files, personality files, target files, and research files; prepares and checks accuracy and completeness of operational, informational, intelligence, and investigative case files and records; supervises special studies, case analyses, and extensive research projects; must be able to perform the duties of Interrogator, 96C30, 96C40 or Military Intelligence Coordinator, 97D30 or 97D40; serves as principal operations NCO in strategic intelligence interrogation center at theater Army or higher headquarters; assists command and staff officers in intelligence operations and training situations for strategic intelligence interrogation activities; collects, prepares, and distributes material and data on intelligence, operations, and training; supervises and inspects duties performed by subordinate noncommissioned officers.
