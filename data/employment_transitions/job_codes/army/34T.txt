Job Code: 34T

Description:
Performs and supervises intermediate maintenance on tactical data communications/computer systems.

Supervises ADP maintenance activities at signal battalion or higher level; supervises maintenance activities in an independent detachment or team supporting ADP center; supervises periodic and emergency maintenance; diagnoses and evaluates complex malfunctions; determines extent of major malfunctions, length of time, tools, and parts required to perform repair; provides technical guidance to maintenance and staff personnel; evaluates quality of maintenance work; instructs personnel in operating and maintenance procedures.

Supervises technical operations and instructs lower grade personnel in performance of maintenance requirements for tactical data communications/ computer systems; supervises ADP maintenance activities at signal battalion or higher level; supervises maintenance activities in an independent detachment or team supporting ADP center; supervises periodic and emergency maintenance; diagnoses and evaluates complex malfunctions; determines extent of major malfunctions, length of time, tools, and parts required to perform repair; provides technical guidance to maintenance and staff personnel; evaluates quality of maintenance work; instructs personnel in operating and maintenance procedures.

Diagnoses malfunctions and performs intermediate maintenance on tactical data communications/computer systems; provides technical guidance to lower grade personnel; performs, supervises, and inspects intermediate maintenance on tactical communications/computer systems and instructs lower grade personnel.
