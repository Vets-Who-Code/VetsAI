Job Code: AWV

Description:
Naval Aircrewmen (Avionics) (AWV) are members of a fixed wing integrated tactical aircrew aboard maritime patrol and reconnaissance, and command and control aircraft; knowledgeable of all avionics systems, safety equipment, emergency procedures, and aircraft equipment.

E4-E6; Able to perform the duties required for E1-E3; Perform primary in-flight and ground duties as aircraft in-flight technicians, Electronic Intelligence (ELINT) specialists, and airborne communicators who maintain and operate aircraft systems.

E7-E8 only, converts to AWCM at E9; Able to perform the duties required for E4-E6; Assess risk; uses tactical communication skills; implements policy; and manages budgets; leads organizational planning.

E1-E3; Assists in the maintenance of aircraft, associated aeronautical equipment, and aircraft support equipment; services, cleans, and handles aircraft; performs other apprentice-level duties involved in the operation of a naval aircraft afloat and ashore.
