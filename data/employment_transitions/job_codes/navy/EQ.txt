Job Code: EQ

Description:
Forecasts future requirements and plans and initiates action to satisfy them; establishes goals and priorities; plans, organizes, implements, and controls work activities in compliance with policy statements and directives.

E9; Able to perform the duties required for either EOCS (Senior Chief Equipment Operator) or CMCS (Senior Chief Construction Mechanic); Reviews personnel, equipment, and material requirements; is responsible for the administration of a department.
