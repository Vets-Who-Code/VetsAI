Job Code: 6310

Description:
Officer managers in aircraft handling and support operations aboard ship. They plan, supervise, and execute the recovery, placement, refueling, and launching of all aircraft aboard CVs, LPHs, and LHAs. They maintain aircraft launch/recovery equipment, visual landing aids, and aviation fuel and lubricant receiving/stowage, and dispensing equipment. They are also charged with aircraft crash and salvage operation and firefighting capabilities. An officer of the Regular Navy whose permanent status is Ensign or above.
