Job Code: 6417

Description:
Officer technical managers who perform duties in the field of administration, personnel, manpower planning and requirement determination studies, organization planning, postal functions and printing. They must have a broad knowledge of administrative and personnel procedures, Navy Department/staff/fleet organizations, regulations, and directives in an array of related assignments. An officer of the Naval Reserve on active duty in the FTS Program (Full Time Support) [Includes officers of the FTS Program rotated to other than FTS billets].
