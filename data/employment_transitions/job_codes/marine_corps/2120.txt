Job Code: 2120

Description:
Weapons repair officers manage the equipment readiness, work progress, and maintenance personnel qualification/training.

They manage the activities of personnel engaged in the recovery, salvage, and maintenance of weapon systems (small arms and artillery). They supervise the eleven functional areas of maintenance management. They manage command environmental and hazardous waste programs. They supervise and provide oversight of disposal, storage, emergency planning for hazardous waste, and ozone depleting substances.
