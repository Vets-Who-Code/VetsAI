Job Code: 9657

Description:
SAM officers plan, direct, coordinate, and supervise the acquisition process including the total life cycle planning of weapons systems/equipment.

Participates as a key project officer in the lifecycle planning and acquisition of weapons systems/equipment; Develops supporting data to justify initiation of validated new systems acquisition programs; Provides planning, programming, and budgeting support to various Marine Corps appropriation sponsors; Develops and evaluates statements of work.
