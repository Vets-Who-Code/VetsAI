Job Code: 6034

Description:
Aircraft nondestructive inspection operators perform liquid penetrant, eddy current, magnetic particle or other methods of NDI as authorized by the Cognizant Field Activity for repetitive Testing of metals in aircraft structures and aircraft engine components.
