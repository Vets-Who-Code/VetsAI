Job Code: 6491

Description:
Precision measurement equipment (PME) chiefs, as noncommissioned officers in charge of Work Center 670, supervise maintenance, calibration, and repair of Test, Measuring, and Diagnostic Equipment (TMDE), IMA weapon system Test Benches, and computer systems to include the ANUYK-65(V).
