Description:

Manage the NIDT Program; Conduct Commanding Officer Briefs; Conduct a MEPS Visit; Manage Recruiting Station Reports Binders; Process Waiver/Service Reviews; Manage the Reserve Referral Program; Direct Applicant Processing For Enlistment

Analyze RSS Mission Restatement Letters; Analyze Monthly RS/RSS Activity Reports; Conduct Mission Planning; Analyze RSS Reports; Conduct RSS Training and Assistance Visit; Conduct a Sales Presentation; Apply Interpersonal communication Skills (IMS)
