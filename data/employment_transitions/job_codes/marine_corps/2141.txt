Job Code: 2141

Description:
The AAV repairer/technician, under supervision, performs basic automotive and hull duties incident to inspection, maintenance, and Repair of the AAV family of vehicles. The AAV repairer/technician also completes basic shop administrative forms and records utilizing technical manuals

Supervise, and perform various duties incident to the inspection, maintenance, and repair of the automotive and turret systems of the AAV; Prepare, maintain and supervise shop maintenance and management programs; Coordinate maintenance and recovery operations according to grade.
