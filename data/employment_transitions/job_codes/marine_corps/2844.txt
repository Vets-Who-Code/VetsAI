Job Code: 2844

Description:
Ground communications organizational repairers are trained to perform modification and organizational level maintenance on ground common radio equipment, ground common telephone equipment, switchboards, peripheral devices, and related electronic equipment used by ground and aviation forces. They are also trained to troubleshoot communications systems to ensure reliable service to users.

Perform ground common transmission systems restoration; Perform corrective maintenance on ground common transmission systems to the shop replaceable unit (SRU) level; Verify the installation of ground common transmission systems
