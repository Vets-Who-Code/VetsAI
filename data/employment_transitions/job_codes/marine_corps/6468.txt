Job Code: 6468

Description:
Aircraft electrical equipment test set technicians, at the IMA level, inspect, test, maintain, and repair airborne weapon replaceable assemblies, Shop replaceable, and ancillary equipment whose aggregate constitutes a complete aircraft weapon system.
