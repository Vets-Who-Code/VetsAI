Job Code: 2300

Description:
Planning, operations, and management related to a very hazardous group of material.

Handles, transports, and stores all type of ammunition, explosives, missiles, inspects materiel to determine serviceability and need for repair or destruction; provides the commander with the capability to neutralize hazards associated with conventional explosive ordnance, weapons of mass destruction (WMD) and improvised explosive devices (IED), and conducts ordnance technical intelligence and dynamic explosive entry.
