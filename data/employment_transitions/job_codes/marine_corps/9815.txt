Job Code: 9815

Description:
Recruiting officers are experienced recruiters who will serve to provide a foundation of recruiting expertise at the recruiting stations, districts, regions, and headquarters. They will serve in the billets outlined in MCO 1100.76.

Compiles and analyzes monthly enlisted recruiting statistics; Coordinates all processing of applicants and shipping of recruits to recruit training; Monitors monthly enlisted recruiting quality indicators; Keeps the commanding officer advised on matters pertaining to the recruiting station's monthly mission attainment; Marine Corps District; Coordinates the training of all recruiting station recruiter instructors; Monitors the training of all recruiters in the district; Advises the commanding officer on all matters pertaining to the training of recruiters; Monitors the monthly enlisted recruiting quality indicators.
