Job Code: 9936

Description:
Substance abuse control officers provide substance abuse education/prevention, urinalysis screening and assistance to the commander on substance abuse related matters. This MOS will be assigned only as a skill designator MOS. Authority to issue this additional MOS will remain with the local command delegated to the unit level.
