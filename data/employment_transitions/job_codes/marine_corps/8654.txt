Job Code: 8654

Description:
Reconnaissance man, parachute and SCUBA/UBA qualified, participates in reconnaissance activities to gain information about the enemy and terrain, employ parachute insertion as the means of entry or when appropriate, SCUBA/UBA to insert/extract.
