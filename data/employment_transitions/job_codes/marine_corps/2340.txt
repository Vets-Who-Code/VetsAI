Job Code: 2340

Description:
Ammunition officers supervise and coordinate ammunition supply and renovation functions. These functions include, but are not limited to, procurement, receipt, storage, issue, handling, shipment, salvage, and renovation of ammunition. They also operate ammunition supply points and are responsible for the establishment of field ammunition supply points in forward areas.

Install hardware; Assemble/Erect and climb utility poles; String wire conductors; Advise commanders on matters relating to the development of ammunition allowances for both training and combat operations.
