Job Code: 8858

Description:
C41 systems officers coordinate the design, development, testing, evaluation, and operation of C41 systems.

Performs advanced systems installation, operation, integration, and troubleshooting in order to maintain optimum secure data communications systems; They plan and supervise the installation, configuration, and maintenance of all data communications systems and network services, in both a garrison and deployed environment; plan and design local and wide area networks and link heterogeneous networks through the application of appropriate data and telecommunications hardware and software;
