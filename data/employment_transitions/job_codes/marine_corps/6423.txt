Job Code: 6423

Description:
Aviation electronic microminiature/instrument and cable repair technicians, at the IMA level, inspect, test, maintain, and repair modules, cards, printed-circuit boards, cables, instruments, and miniature and microminiature components They also perform appropriate level of corrosion control.

Inspect modules, cards, printed-circuit boards, cables, instruments, and miniature and microminiature components; Test modules, cards, printed-circuit boards, cables, instruments, and miniature and microminiature components; Maintain modules, cards, printed-circuit boards, cables, instruments, and miniature and microminiature components; Repair modules, cards, printed-circuit boards, cables, instruments, and miniature and microminiature components; Perform appropriate level of corrosion control
