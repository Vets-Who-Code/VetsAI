Fuse All Source Information; Conduct Step 1-4 of the IPB Process; Conduct System Administration for the Intelligence Analysis System (IAS) Family of Systems (FOS); Review Outgoing Intelligence Reports; Incorporate Emerging intelligence Technology Systems; Provide Intelligence Support to Joint Operations; Provide Intelligence Support to Multi-National Operations
