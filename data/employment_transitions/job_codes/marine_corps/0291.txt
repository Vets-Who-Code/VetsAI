Job Code: 0291

Description:
Intelligence chiefs assist and advise the AC/S, G-2 concerning the operation of the intelligence section. Their responsibilities are to ensure efficient operation of the intelligence section by supervising, instructing, and assisting section personnel in the performance of their duties. This MOS applies to E-9/ MGySgt.

Facilitate Coordination with the Intelligence Community and external agencies; Supervise Intelligence Support to the Marine Corps Planning Process (MCPP); Manage Human Resources; Manage Intelligence Structure; Manage Fiscal Resources
