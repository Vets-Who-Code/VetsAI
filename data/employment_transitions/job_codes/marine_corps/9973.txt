Job Code: 9973

Description:
This is a temporary skill designator to identify the enlisted ground maintenance technician and aircrew member training during the introduction of the KC-130J. This MOS will also identify pilots and copilots for the KC-130J. Specific officer and enlisted primary MOSs will be designated after sufficient aircraft fielding increases.
