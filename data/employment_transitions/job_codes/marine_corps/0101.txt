Job Code: 0101

Description:
Basic Personnel and Administration Marine will supervise routine personnel and administrative functions while training for a designated MOS within the OccFld.

The duties involve administrative, managerial, and technical skills. Personnel administration and retention Marines are required to learn clerical and administrative procedures, office management, personal computer skills (personnel and pay database retrieval and wordprocessing), preparation and use of military publications and correspondence, preparation of orders and directives, and the use of filing systems and record keeping.
