Job Code: 6431

Description:
Aircraft electrical systems technician-trainees train for a specific Aircraft Electrical Systems Technician MOS through formal school training and/or OJT, and under close supervision/instruction perform routine duties relative to inspection, maintenance, and repair of aircraft electrical systems at the IMA level.
