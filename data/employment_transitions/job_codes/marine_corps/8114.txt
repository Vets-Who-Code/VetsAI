Job Code: 8114

Description:
The rigid raiding craft (RRC) coxswain is responsible for the safety and conduct of his boat team and the safe operation, handling, launch, recovery and maintenance of Rigid Raiding Craft (RRC) and its associated equipment, components and SL-3 items.
